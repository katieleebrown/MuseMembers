About MuseMembers

MuseMembers was created by Katie Brown, a software engineer with a history of working in museums and cultural institutions. After hearing years of feedback from members and non-members in her organization, Katie realized she could take that input to design a CMS built on these fundamental ideas - letting members access their own information, giving members easy ways to skip the check in lines, and automating some of the tasks that added extra work onto the already full plates of front-line museum staff. MuseMembers truly is a CMS built by museum professionals, for museum professionals.

MuseMembers has been a long work in progress, and is currently in it's beta testing phase. If you're interested in providing feedback for MuseMembers, or have additional questions about our system, please use our contact form below! Please note: beta testing priority is given to North Carolina cultural institutions.
