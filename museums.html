Features For Museums About Us

Why MuseMembers?

A Customer Management System designed with your members in mind.

MuseMembers is a Customer Management System for museums and cultural institutions. MuseMembers works with you to provide more benefits for your members and your staff. Looking for more member benefits? Our on-site check in lets your members skip the line and get inside faster. Looking to keep memberships up to date without extra work for your staff? We put the data into the hands of your members so they are in control. They can update their information in real time, upgrade their membership level, and renew their membership when time is running short. Additionally, you can use our member portal to share vital information about your organization with all of your members simultaneously. MuseMembers is here to expand your reach to give you more time to do what you do best - inspire your visitors.

More benefits to increase your user base

As a cultural institution, you're always looking for ways to increase your membership base. MuseMembers helps you do that by providing more membership perks. Letting members check in on-site and skip the line is one of our highest rated features, proven to boost membership sign ups. We use location tracking to guarantee your members are on site, and include a short questionairre to determine how many people are entering under the membership.

Accessible information to increase customer retention

Are you looking for more ways to keep your members coming back year after year? We've seen that giving members real-time access to their membership, with quick and easy ways to update, extend, and renew is one of the best ways to keep your members coming back. By putting the membership into their hands - literally, we're mobile friendly - your members will take ownership, keeping their data and membership up-to-date.

Automated messaging to save your staff time

As a cultural institution, we know your staff are working tirelessly to provide the best experience possible. Let us help you give them time back in their day by automating membership renwals for you! We send automatic reminders to your members one month, two weeks, and the week of their membership expiration date. We also give you a platform to share essential information with all your members, including site maps, weather updates, and your annual calendar.
