MuseMembers Features

Manage Your Membership

MuseMembers gives you easy access to all of your membership information.

Just moved and need to update your address? Had a new addition to the family and need to increase the number of guests on your membership? Ready to renew for another year?

Your information is always accessible and editable to keep you up-to-date and in the know about what data your museum has access to. No more waiting at the front desk to update, make changes in real time on your phone or computer.

Check In On-Site

Looking for extra perks from your membership? Hate waiting in line just to scan your card?

MuseMembers lets you check in on site and skip the line so you can get inside faster. Once on site, sign in to MuseMembers and click 'Check In'. You'll need to let us know how many guests you're bringing in with you, and then you're all set! Get to the fun, faster.

Get Updates When You Need Them

MuseMembers helps you keep all of your information in one place. Alongside your membership information, you can also access essential documents and announcements from your museum.

Are you looking for a site map before you visit? Want to check the calendar of events? Need to check the status of the institution due to inclement weather? You can find all of this information under the 'Museum' tab when you sign in to MuseMembers.

MuseMembers will also send you updates when it's time to renew your membership, review your data annually, or access additional perks from your museum.
